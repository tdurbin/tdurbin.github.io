Get housing advice

Answer a few questions to get advice about:

  • how to solve problems affecting your housing situation
  • what to do if you have been asked to leave your property
  • how you can find somewhere else to live
  • how we can help you.

You don’t need to give any personal details about who you are or where you live.
 

Worried about homelessness?

You should contact Lewisham Council’s Housing Team straight away if you are homeless now, or are likely to become homeless within the next 56 days.
